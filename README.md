# 600091_HSP_Emma_Hodkin
## Honours Stage Project: Student motivation (NAG-25-949) 
### HSP Synopsis:
The aim of this project is to develop an app to encourage and support students in their studies. It could include some or all of the following themes: a) motivation, with techniques from gamification such as applied games mechanics, e.g. for rewards and progress. b) well being - this may take the form of links to other university information sources, as well as including various elements of functionality: including diary and time management, and stress and more general wellbeing support c) organisation and planning - aggregate information from existing university information systems (especially timetabling, campus virtual learning environments (e.g. Canvas) etc. to offer a bespoke mobile software system capable of supporting students d) specific support to aid assessment management. This could be a variant of the calendar/diary - though more likely some form of task planner e) support for those with special educational needs, with links to campus and wider support resources for different needs.
